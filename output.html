    Guarantee output

    Toeic's output program is also very different. Through this article, Fum wants to redefine what is to ensure the output, the factors that help a student to achieve the expected output.

    Guarantee output

    This is a concept that many students are being led leading to a very serious misunderstanding:
    Toeic is ✊
    First, Ensuring the output must be guaranteed the quality of teaching so that all students of the course achieve the output goals, not the study again until they achieve the output.
    Second, Make sure the output must be in accordance with the input ability of each student, not to lower the input of the course to reach the output with the right output to ensure the output.
    Usually is 🤡
    Make sure the output of teaching is great, then let's learn until the output is achieved. This type of learning is also boring, takes time, and does not bring anything.
    Therefore, at Toeic, there is no policy to ensure the learning output until the output is achieved.
    Toeic believes with Linearthinking and learning experience at Toeic, serious learning students will surely achieve the output.
    However, in case some of you are unlucky to take the exam due to psychological, Toeic has a policy to support after the course by you by the way.:
    Arrange 1 session 1-1 with teachers to analyze why not, find the cause and give a separate route
