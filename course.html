    • Courses at Toeic Education

    Toeic is the only unit to build a comprehensive program to help students hunt scholarships to top universities. Proud of Toeic students honoring Mit, Caltech, UC Berkeley, UCLA, Cornell…

    The diverse training program is suitable for the needs of students such as IELTS exam preparation course, or TOEFL, SAT, ACT, Teenage IELTS, … and Essay strategy & Submit the scholarship hunting application (AMP).

    Toeic's program

    AMP scholarship hunting

    Essay strategy & Applying AMP scholarships is a 1 -intensive training program throughout the scholarship hunting scholarship roadmap at the top universities of the US, Australia, Canada and Singapore.

    IELTS Super Intensive

    Ensure output - IELTS 6.5 after 8 weeks. Having basic knowledge about the structure and approach to the IELTS test Forming the right to learn English thinking. Completely remove reading, translating, speaking. Ability to express and develop their ideas

    IELTS Online

    Learning IELTS online as offline with Linearthinking method, dedicated teacher, supportive technology platform, ensuring output.

    PRE IELTS

    Orienting how to learn English properly, from which becomes more interested in learning. Understand how to learn vocabulary/ grammar effectively. Ability to connect words to write a complete sentence Accumulate the most basic vocabulary for each topic applicable to listening - speaking - reading - writing skills

    TOEFL

    The course has many levels from basic to advanced. Equipped with knowledge and skills to use English effectively in the US

    SAT

    SAT course for students from 15 years old, increasing the competitive advantage in the application for scholarships to study by SAT teachers

    IELTS

    IELTS course for students from 14 years old taught by former IELTS judges. Commitment to comprehensive development of 4 English skills and increased bands of IELTS destination from 7.0

    TOEIC

    The course focuses on strengthening and expanding the basic knowledge foundation for students, and for students to get acquainted and apply Linearthinking. Achieving TOEIC target from 550-750+ after only 24 sessions

    IELTS Juniors

    Building and comprehensive development of academic English platform. Commit to achieve IELTS 6.5 at the end of grade 9 (*)

    English communication

    The course focuses on strengthening the basic English knowledge platform for students, and for students to get acquainted with popular communication topics. Since then, forming communication reflexes, knowing how to read the situation of communication and communication naturally

    Writing Speaking 8.0+

Ability to express and develop their ideas quickly and fluently Accumulates high -end vocabulary and grammar and can be applied in Writing & Speaking
